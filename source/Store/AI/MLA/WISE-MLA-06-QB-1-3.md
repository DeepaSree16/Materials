# Question 1

What is the output of the below code?

````
import numpy as np
a = np.array([11,12,31])
b = np.array([34,35,36])
c = np.hstack((a, b))
print(c)

````
### Options

A. [34 35 36 11 12 31]
B. [11 12 31 34 35 36]
C. [34 35 36]

**Answer:** B
