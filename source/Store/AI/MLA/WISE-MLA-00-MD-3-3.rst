ML 201 Module Topic-wise Coverage
=================================

Focus
-----

* Maths prerequisite for Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

Topic-wise Coverage
-------------------

Module 1
++++++++

:Focus: Tool chain for ML
:Duration: 6 Hours
:Agenda:
    * Sage Math
    * Environment 
	- Anaconda, Jupyter Notebook, Colaboratory 
    * Python Libraries
	- Pandas, Numpy, Scipy and Matplotlib

Module 2
+++++++++

:Focus: Foundation of Maths
:Duration: 6 hours
:Agenda:

    * Linear Algebra
	- Matrix
	- Vectors 
    * Calculus
	- Derivatives
	- Chain rule
	- Integrals

Module 3
+++++++++

:Focus: Statistics and data interpretation 
:Duration: 12 hours
:Agenda:
    * Sampling and Empirical Distributions
    * Why the Mean Matters?
    * Estimation
    * Testing Hypotheses

Module 4
+++++++++

:Focus: Introduction to ML
:Duration: 12 hours
:Agenda: 
     * What is ML?
     * Supervised, Unsupervised, Semi-supervised, and Reinforcement Learning 
     * Terminology
     * Pipeline for ML
     * Training, Validation and Testing
