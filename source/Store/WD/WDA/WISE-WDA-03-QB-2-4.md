# Question 1

Which of the following is the correct way to apply a filter?

### Options

a. {{ expression | filter1 | filter2 | ... }}
b. {{ {filter1} | {filter2} | ...-expression}}
c. {{ expression | {filter1} | {filter2} | ... }}
d. None of the above

**Answer:** a
